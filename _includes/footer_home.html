About Words and Magic

I started this project as a place to write. I didn't know which direction I wanted to go when I started it, but it was inpsired by Alex Charchar's An Essay a Day project. Now, I focus on storytelling, be that in art, graphic design, music, video, movies, games or well... anything really. I love the stories behind everything.
